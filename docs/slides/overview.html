Course Outline

Notes

  • Week 1: Basics of module theory: definition, sub/quotient modules, mapping properties (DF 10.1-10.2). notes slides
  • Week 2: More module theory: sums, products, free modules, decomposition (DF 10.3)
  • Week 3: Finitely generated modules over PIDs (DF 12.1)
  • Week 4: Canonical Forms (DF 12.2-12.3)
  • Week 5: Field theory basics: algebraic/transcendental extensions, field degree (DF 13.1-13.2)
  • Week 6: Field extensions: composites, characteristic polynomial, trace, norm, separability (DF 13.5)
  • Week 7: Galois Theory (DF 14.1-14.2)
  • Week 8: Applications and Examples of Galois Theory
  • Week 9: Tensor products (DF 10.4)
  • Week 10: Base extension; exterior algebras (DF 10.4, 11.5)

Other topics to be decided later.
